<product_requirements>

Key features include dynamic fields for cost, end date, and auto-generated contract numbers, with output in a formatted Word document (Times New Roman 11pt, within 3 pages, consistent signature alignment). Specific formatting rules apply: Казань top-left, Moscow-time signing date top-right, Section 11 on a new page with compact spacing, and a prominent (14pt, bold, centered) contract title. All dates must use Moscow time.

3.  **Filename Format**: Downloaded files must adhere to Договор для [Organization/Full Name in Genitive Case].docx.
4.  **Internal Title Integrity**: The internal Word document title (Договор об оказании услуг № КР...) must remain unchanged, regardless of external edits or display.

<product_requirements>
The initial product was a contract management service with a default contract template, client data fields, and the ability to auto-populate customer sections. Enhancements included downloading contracts in Word format (preserving structure) and simplifying the client form to Name/organization name and Other data.

The new user requirements drastically shifted the focus:
1.  **Unified Page:** The application should operate on a single page.
2.  **Contract Fields:**
    *   Add a Стоимость (Cost) field.
    *   Automatically generate Стоимость прописью (Cost in words) without a dedicated input field.
    *   Add a Дата окончания договора (Contract End Date) field with options: month, half-year, year (defaulting to half-year).
    *   Automatically generate Номер договора (Contract Number) based on the creation date (e.g., № КР22.08.25).
3.  **Contract Display & Interaction:** Upon creation, the contract should appear below the form, viewable, editable, and downloadable.
4.  **Client Management Removal:** The saving clients functionality is no longer needed.
5.  **Word Document Formatting (Post-development feedback):** The downloaded Word document must adhere to specific formatting: Times New Roman, size 11, fit within 3 pages, and maintain signature fields at a consistent level.
</product_requirements>
